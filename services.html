OVERVIEW

One of the aims of the Smart Meter roll-out which commenced in 2015 is to improve operational efficiency by bringing an end to estimated billing. This ongoing program, however, introduces with it a new risk with respect to the security and privacy of users’ personally identifiable information.
In order to assure all stakeholders that their information security and data privacy rights are upheld, the DECC has therefore mandated that all suppliers comply with a new industry code, the Smart Energy Code. This will help to ensure that end-to-end security can be addressed as part of the roll-out process from the outset.

WHY STAR STRATEGOS?

At Star Strategos, we understand the specific challenges posed to Energy Suppliers as the roll-out process gets underway. Our consultants are industry-certified experts and can help your organization achieve ISO 27001 compliance as well as effectively prepare for both the Foundation & Enduring Smart Metering Audits by the Competent Independent Organisation (CIO). This we do by reviewing and assessing your security designs, controls, policies, procedures ahead of your audit.
As part of our Smart Metering Services, we provide all our clients with:
On-demand Subject Matter Experts in both Foundation & Enduring Smart metering to help them meet their business objectives. Expertise in ISO 27001:2013, ISO27002, ISO 27005, ISO 27035, BS 7858, SEC Standards and Obligations. Deliverables based on industry proven best practices. Deliverables which satisfy the enterprise’s regulatory and compliance responsibilities.
